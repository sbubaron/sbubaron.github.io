Recess, For Adults.

LI-Kick is Long Island's premier provider of co-ed adult social sports. Our mission is to provide a fun and social experience for adults on Long Island. We pride ourselves on offering strong recreational and competitive platforms for players to strengthen friendships & meet new people, all while playing a really fun game. The league offers the structure of a professional sports league while combining the social aspect of bar socials and sponsored events throughout the season.

Our Leagues

Kickball

We play kickball 8 months a year across 5 different levels of play. Spring Kickball (Apr/May) and Fall Kickball (Sept/Oct) are the 2 marquee leagues and sell out with over 300 players in each league. The Super League (Jul) is Long Island's most competitive league. The Draft League (Nov) is a great way to meet new people and play on new teams. Finally we have indoor play in the Winter (Jan/Feb)

Next League Starts: 1/5/2017

Soccer

We run 4 soccer leagues throughout the year- Spring (Mar/Apr), Summer (Jul/Aug), Fall (Sept/Oct), and Indoor (Dec/Jan). All leagues are coed and played on junior size fields under the supervision of a qualified referee.

Next League Starts: 1/5/2017

Dodgeball Coming Soon

Dodgeball is organized indoors during the long, cold winter nights. The league is 'pick-up' style and players can join for the full season or play on a week-by-week basis

Weekly Open Nights Starting: 1/25/2017

Bar Room Heros

Social sports aren't about achieving sporting greatness. We offer league members the opportunity to engage in a fun activity with friends, meet new people, get involved and be active. Every night we take the fun off the field to organized after-parties at local bars for you to share a drink with your teammates and opponents.
